Privacy Policy

For IRI National, IRI Productions, and Luv Media Group

Last updated:4/29/2026

1. Who we are

This unified Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how IRI National (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you interact with our ecosystem of brands and experiences.

IRI National operates the following “doing business as” entities (collectively, the “Services”):

  • IRI National – parent company and operator of the broader xSeries ecosystem.
  • IRI Productions – public-facing creative and media production brand under IRI National.
  • Luv Media Group – curated, sensual-adjacent media and experiences, designed to remain tasteful, consensual, and safe.

When this Policy refers to “you”, it means any visitor, viewer, customer, or collaborator who accesses or uses any of the Services.

2. Scope of this policy

This Policy applies to information we collect through our websites, applications, digital products, content experiences, communication channels, and any other online or offline touchpoints that link to or reference this Policy.

Brand-specific nuances are highlighted where relevant:

  • IRI National – governance, infrastructure, and account-level operations.
  • IRI Productions – creative content, media projects, and production-related interactions.
  • Luv Media Group – sensual-adjacent, adult-oriented experiences with additional age and consent safeguards.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

We may collect information you choose to share with us, including:

  • Contact details – name, email address, social handles, and similar identifiers.
  • Account information – usernames, passwords, profile preferences, and settings.
  • Communication content – messages, inquiries, feedback, and support requests.
  • Transaction details – limited purchase-related information (e.g., products or services ordered, timestamps, and basic billing metadata).
  • Creative and collaboration data – submissions, briefs, or materials you share for projects with IRI Productions.
  • Preference and consent signals – opt-ins, opt-outs, and content preferences, especially for Luv Media Group experiences.

3.2 Information collected automatically

When you interact with our Services, we may automatically collect certain technical and usage information, such as:

  • Device and browser data – IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
  • Usage data – pages viewed, links clicked, session duration, and navigation patterns.
  • Approximate location – derived from your IP address, where permitted by law.
  • Log data – system logs, error reports, and diagnostic information.

3.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the experience smooth, responsive, and tailored to your preferences. These may include:

  • Essential cookies – required for core functionality and security.
  • Performance and analytics cookies – help us understand how the Services are used.
  • Preference cookies – remember your settings, themes, and content choices.

Where required by law, we will request your consent before using non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

3.4 Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from third-party platforms or partners, for example:

  • Payment processors – limited transaction confirmations and billing metadata (we do not store full payment card numbers).
  • Analytics providers – aggregated insights about traffic and engagement.
  • Social or identity providers – if you choose to sign in or connect via a third-party account.

4. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining the Services – operating sites, apps, and content experiences.
  • Personalizing your experience – remembering your preferences, themes, and content choices.
  • Communicating with you – responding to messages, sending updates, and service-related notices.
  • Processing transactions – fulfilling purchases, subscriptions, or bookings.
  • Improving and securing the Services – analytics, testing, troubleshooting, and security monitoring.
  • Complying with legal obligations – recordkeeping, regulatory requirements, and lawful requests.

For Luv Media Group, we may additionally use your information to:

  • Respect your boundaries – honor your content preferences and opt-outs.
  • Maintain a safe environment – apply age and consent safeguards, and prevent misuse or abuse.

5. Legal bases for processing (where applicable)

Where required by applicable law (such as in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom), we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide Services you request.
  • Legitimate interests – to operate, secure, and improve our Services in a balanced way.
  • Consent – for certain communications, cookies, or optional features.
  • Legal obligations – to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in limited ways, including:

  • Within the IRI National ecosystem – among IRI National, IRI Productions, and Luv Media Group, where needed to operate a unified experience.
  • Service providers – trusted vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, communications, payments, or security, under appropriate contractual safeguards.
  • Legal and safety reasons – when required by law, or when we believe it is necessary to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the Services.
  • Business transfers – in connection with a merger, acquisition, or similar corporate event, subject to continued protection of your information.

7. Payments and third-party processors

When you make a purchase or subscription, payments are typically processed by third-party payment processors. These processors handle your payment card details directly and operate under their own privacy and security practices.

We may receive limited billing-related information (such as transaction IDs, timestamps, and partial details) to confirm payment and maintain records, but we do not store full payment card numbers on our own systems.

8. Age and eligibility

8.1 General Services

Our general Services are intended for individuals who are at least the age of majority in their jurisdiction or who are using the Services with appropriate parental or guardian consent, where permitted by law.

8.2 Luv Media Group

Luv Media Group is designed for adults only and is not intended for individuals under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, if higher).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors in connection with Luv Media Group. If we learn that a minor has accessed or provided information to Luv Media Group, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information and restrict access.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, which can include:

  • Access – request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction – request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion – request that we delete certain personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Restriction – request that we limit certain processing activities.
  • Objection – object to certain processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Consent withdrawal – withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.

To exercise these rights, you can contact us using the details in the Contact us section below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No system is perfectly secure, but we aim to keep your data handled with care and intention.

12. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (for example, for tax, accounting, or legal obligations).

13. International transfers

Our Services may be accessed from around the world. If your information is transferred across borders, we will take steps to ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, consistent with applicable law.
